Corona virus Awareness Page
To prevent the spread of COVID-19:

1.Clean your hands often.     2.Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.    3.Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.    4.Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.    5.Dont touch your eyes, nose or mouth.    6.Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.    7.Stay home if you feel unwell.    8.If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention.
